About Us

We are a group of Universidade de Aveiro MIECT students, and together we form group 13 of Software Engineering course, mentored by Professor José Maria.

This is the official site for our project BitBusTracker, in wich we are going to make a system that allows real time monitoring of the position of buses in the city of Porto, and present it in a real time interactive map application.
